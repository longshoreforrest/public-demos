Miksi tämä sovellus on tehty?

AI-teknologiayrityksessä työntekijät suorittavat sertifikaatteja kymmeniltä eri tarjoajilta — AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Anthropic, OpenAI ja monet muut. Kenelläkään ei ole kokonaiskuvaa siitä, kuka on suorittanut mitäkin, miten strategiset tavoitteet etenevät (esim. kumppanuusstatukset) tai mitä sertifikaatteja kannattaisi priorisoida. SerttiApuri kokoaa kaiken yhteen: katalogi, tavoitteet, analytiikka ja henkilökohtaiset oppimispolut.

Näin pääset alkuun

  1. Sovellus avautuu Dashboard-näkymään jossa näet yhteenvedon organisaation sertifikaattitilanteesta.
  2. Tutustu Katalogi-näkymään selataksesi yli 60 sertifikaattia ja suodattaaksesi tarjoajan, tason tai tyypin mukaan.
  3. Tarkista Tavoitteet-näkymästä yrityksen strategiset sertifikaattitavoitteet ja niiden edistyminen.
  4. Etsi kollegoita Henkilöhaku-näkymästä tai suunnittele omaa urapolkuasi Oma polku -näkymässä.

HR-päällikkö

Sertifikaattitarjoajat

Hyödyllistä tietää

  • Sovellus sisältää yli 60 sertifikaattia ja noin 100 henkilön profiilit demoa varten.
  • Kaikki data on generoitua — henkilöt, suoritukset ja tavoitteet ovat fiktiivisiä.
  • AI-assistentti on demo-toteutus eikä käytä oikeaa tekoälymallia.
  • Sertifikaattien hinnat ja kestot perustuvat todellisiin markkinahintoihin (2024–2025).
  • Tarjoajien brändivärit (AWS oranssi, Anthropic ruskea jne.) helpottavat visuaalista tunnistamista.
  • Sovellus toimii kokonaan selaimessa ilman palvelinta.

Why was this app built?

In an AI technology company, employees earn certifications from dozens of different providers — AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Anthropic, OpenAI and many others. No one has a clear picture of who has completed what, how strategic goals are progressing (e.g. partnership statuses) or which certifications should be prioritized. SerttiApuri brings everything together: catalog, goals, analytics and personalized learning paths.

Getting started

  1. The app opens to the Dashboard view where you see a summary of the organization's certification status.
  2. Explore the Catalog view to browse over 60 certifications and filter by provider, level or type.
  3. Check the Goals view for the company's strategic certification targets and their progress.
  4. Find colleagues in the People view or plan your career path in the My Path view.

Nine views

Overview

Dashboard

A high-level summary of the organization's certification status.

  • Stat cards: headcount, completed certifications, certifications in the catalog and active goals.
  • Charts: distributions by provider, level and team.
  • A quick overview of the organization's competency landscape.
Company

Goals

Strategic certification targets and their tracking.

  • Company-level goals such as "10 Anthropic Partner certifications" or "80% of developers AI-trained".
  • Progress bars show how close you are to each target.
  • Deadlines and responsible persons for each goal.
  • Click the "completed" count to see who has already passed.
Company

Analytics

In-depth analytics on certifications across the entire organization.

  • Distribution by provider, level, team and cost.
  • Drill down into individual certifications and their holders.
  • Trends and developments over time.
Certifications

Catalog

A comprehensive directory of all available certifications.

  • Over 60 certifications from providers: AWS, Microsoft, Google, Anthropic, OpenAI, NVIDIA, GitHub, Databricks and more.
  • Filter by provider, level (Foundational/Associate/Professional/Expert), type and price.
  • Search by name or description.
  • Each certification shows price, duration, level and how many people have completed it.
Certifications

Tips & Resources

Colleague tips for completing certifications.

  • Practical exam tips: difficulty level, preparation time, best study materials.
  • Time estimates and recommended study strategies.
  • Links to official resources and study materials.
People

People Search

Find colleagues and their certifications.

  • Search by name, team or certification.
  • Each person shows team, role and completed certifications.
  • Click a person to see their detailed profile.
People

My Path

Your personal certification path and recommendations.

  • Recommended certifications based on your role and team.
  • Estimates of cost and time to complete the path.
  • Prioritization according to strategic goals.
People

Leaderboard

Ranking of certification achievers.

  • Staff ranked by number of completed certifications.
  • Shows certification count, level and most recent completion.
  • Motivates and gives visibility to active learners.
People

AI Assistant

A conversational helper for certification questions.

  • Ask anything about certifications, learning paths or goals.
  • Get personalized recommendations based on your situation.
  • A demo implementation showcasing what an AI-assisted experience could look like.
Tip: Start from the Catalog view and filter by provider "Anthropic" — you will immediately see which certifications the partnership status requires and how many people have already completed them. This helps you understand the organization's progress toward strategic goals.

Use cases

HR Manager

"How are certification goals progressing?"

Open Goals → see company-level targets with progress bars. Drill into the Analytics view to see the breakdown by team and identify where more effort is needed.

Developer

"Which certification should I take next?"

Open My Path → see recommended certifications based on your role and team. Check Tips & Resources for colleagues' experiences before you start studying.

Team Lead

"Who on my team has AWS expertise?"

Open People Search → filter by provider "AWS" → see everyone with AWS certifications. Or open Analytics and drill down to the team level.

CTO

"Do we have enough AI expertise for partnerships?"

Open Goals → check partnership goal progress (e.g. "Anthropic Partner"). Go to the Dashboard for the big picture and the Analytics view for provider-level drill-downs.

Certification providers

The app covers certifications from the following providers:

  • Cloud platforms: AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud
  • AI platforms: Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI, NVIDIA, DeepLearning.AI
  • Dev tools: GitHub, Cursor, HashiCorp
  • Data & CRM: Databricks, Salesforce

Certification levels: Foundational (entry level), Associate (intermediate), Professional (advanced) and Expert (specialist).

Good to know

  • The app contains over 60 certifications and approximately 100 employee profiles for demo purposes.
  • All data is generated — people, completions and goals are fictional.
  • The AI assistant is a demo implementation and does not use a real AI model.
  • Certification prices and durations are based on actual market prices (2024–2025).
  • Provider brand colors (AWS orange, Anthropic brown, etc.) aid visual identification.
  • The app runs entirely in the browser with no server required.
